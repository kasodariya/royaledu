Why MBBS Study in Russia?

Key Features of MBBS in Russia


★ Russian Medical Universities Ranking- 30 positions amongst top 100 ranking Medical Universities according to the World Health Organization (WHO)'s "Directory of World Medical Schools".

★ Renowned Alumni - Pass-outs from Russian Medical Universities are presently working in the leading Hospitals across the globe.

★ Global Affiliated, Accredited, and Recognised Universities - All universities are recognised by WHO & Medical Council of leading countries like the U.S.A, U.K, Middle East, Australia, India, Canada, etc.

★ Subsidised fee structure- Most of the universities are aided by the Russian government. Thus, the fee is subsidised by the Russian Ministry of Health & Education makes education affordable to all.

★Resourceful universities- Russia is home to almost 57 resourceful universities in terms of infrastructure, technical advances, qualified lecturers, etc

★ Course Duration- The duration of MBBS in Russia in English Medium is 6years. Students can also opt for MBBS in Russian Medium which is of 7 years with the addition of 1 year as Russian Language Training (Preparatory Faculty).

Reasons to study MBBS in Russia


☛ Affordable and feasible study- Russian government put the subsidy of approximately 70% for the MBBS courses in Russian Universities. The average MBBS fee in Russia is between 2.5 Lacs to 5 Lacs per year

☛ High FMGE Result- Most of the Russian Universities provide MCI Screening Test Coaching on the university's premises itself. As a result, graduates show excellent results in the FMGE exam and landed themselves among the best hospitals and industries in India and abroad.

☛ Technically Equipped Learning- MBBS in Russia is aided by the state-of-the-art equipment and advanced teaching methods known by the medical universities across the globe.

☛ Numerous Specialisation Course- Russian Medical Universities provide the students an ample number, of course, to choose from and specialise in. There are more than 100 fields from which the student can choose to specialise in.

☛ Goal-Oriented Learning- MBBS in Russia features ways of imparting education in a regulated and streamlined method that focuses on the extensive skill development in the specialties the student chooses.
